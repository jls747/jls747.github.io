Watch at home

The Discoverers is now available on select digital platforms, including Amazon and iTunes!

We’re excited about our special edition DVD with a behind-the-scenes audio commentary with writer/director Justin Schwarz and producer Laura Kleger. Get the inside scoop on our cast, shooting in the woods during deer mating season, lighting candles and custom fire-bars, making a handmade score, and how our picture car was inspired by a William Christenberry photo.
